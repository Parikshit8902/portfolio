INTERNSHIPS



1. Research Intern - Indian Space Research Organization(ISRO). (on-site: 15/02/24 - 15/06/24)

I had the opportunity to work as a Firmware Development Intern at ISRO Space Application Centre, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India.

I joined them for the duration of 4 months. My primary task was to design algorithms for USB 3.0 based High Speed Data Acquisition Systems. A Data Acquisition System is a ground-based system, which communicates with Satellites as well as airborne payloads.

During the tenure of my internship, I developed programs in C/C++ for enabling I2C, SPI, UART as well as Parallel-Data-Bus Transfer protocols for efficient communication. Along with implementing them on the Hardware, I was also responsible for debugging and thorough testing of the Boards. I got the opportunity to work with Scientists and experts in the domain.



2. GALANTO INNOVATIONS PVT. LTD. (on-site: 10/08/23 - 10/09/23)

Galanto Innovations is a rising organisation in the domain of MedTech. Based in Research Park of IIT Gandhinagar, the company is focused on developing Rehabilitation devices which would take rehabilitation to the next level by gamifying the exercises.Their latest poduct being the RehabRelieve glove, where one can play games on their software while performing hand exercise.

I joined them for the duration of a month, as an Embedded Systems intern. Where I was assigned task to develop PCB for rehabilitation devices, along with working on Sensors to develop and test algorithms to track precise movements of the parts of the body.

I also had the opportunity to represent the organisation at the All India MedTech Expo 2023 held at Helipad Exhibition Center in Gandhinagar, Gujarat. I got the opportunity to connect with a lot of Angel Investors, Businessmen, Medicos as well as Tech Geeks!
